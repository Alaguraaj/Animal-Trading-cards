Tiger

They are strong, powerful and one of nature's most feared predators.

  • Scientific name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 70-120cm
  • Average Lifespan: 20-26 years
  • Habitat: Rain forersts,grasslands, savannas

Tigers are the largest member of the cat family. Their beautiful orange and black striped coats provide camouflage when hunting prey at night when they can reach speeds of 65 km/hr (~40 mph). The tiger has a muscular body with powerful forelimbs, a large head and a tail that is about half the length of its body.
